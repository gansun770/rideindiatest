Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026. Your privacy matters to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through the RideIndia website, mobile applications, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, driving license details, and payment information when you create an account or make a booking. We also automatically collect certain technical data such as your IP address, device type, and browsing activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, verify your identity, provide customer support, prevent fraud, personalize your experience, and send you important service updates. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

  • To confirm reservations and manage your rentals.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • To improve our platform and develop new features.

3. Sharing With Third Parties

We share information only with trusted partners who help us operate our services, including:

  • Secure payment gateways and processing networks.
  • Identity verification & anti-fraud assessment bureaus.
  • Local roadside assistance, mechanical towing operators, and vehicle insurance underwriters.

4. Data Security

We implement bank-grade encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You may exercise these rights at any time by contacting our Data Protection Officer.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related enquiries, please email privacy@rideindia.in or write to our Data Protection Officer at the office address on our contact page.
